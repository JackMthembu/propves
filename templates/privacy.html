Propves Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2024-10-27

This Privacy Policy describes how Propves ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects the personal information of landlords, tenants, and other individuals who use our AI-powered property management platform ("Platform") in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA) of South Africa.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal information:

Landlord Information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, physical address, password (securely hashed and salted).
  • Property Information: Address, type of property, number of units, photos, rental amount, lease terms.
  • Financial Information: Bank account details for rent collection, payment history.
  • Tenant Information: As provided by the tenant through our Platform, including contact information, lease details, and payment history.
  • Communication Data: Records of your communications with us (e.g., emails, support tickets).

Tenant Information:

  • Information provided directly by tenants via our marketplace: When tenants use our marketplace to find a property, we collect the following information:
    • Contact information: Name, email address, phone number, physical address.
    • Employment information: Current employer, job title, and salary for pre-qualification purposes.
    • Affordability information: Information related to your income and expenses to assess your ability to pay rent.
  • Identification Information: For identity verification purposes, we may collect a copy of your ID document (e.g., National ID, passport).
  • Lease Information: Lease agreement details, rental amount, payment history.
  • Financial Information: Bank account details for rent payments.
  • Conduct Information: With your explicit consent, information about your rental payment history, communication with the landlord, and property maintenance.
  • Communication Data: Records of your communications with us and your landlord through the Platform (e.g., messages, maintenance requests).

Other Information:

  • Website Usage Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages visited, and date/time stamps.
  • Device Information: Device type, unique device identifiers, operating system.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to track your activity on our Platform and hold certain information. You can learn more about our use of cookies in our Cookie Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and Improving the Platform:
    • To facilitate lease management, invoicing, account reconciliation, tenant placement, viewing bookings, and payment collection.
    • To generate lease agreements, automate renewals, and manage lease enforcement.
    • To automatically re-advertise properties and manage tenant turnover.
    • To provide personalized recommendations and support to landlords and tenants.
  • Tenant Pre-qualification and Matching:
    • To assess tenant suitability for landlords based on the information provided by tenants through the marketplace.
    • To connect tenants with properties that meet their needs and affordability.
  • Rewards Program: To administer the rewards system and encourage timely rent payments.
  • Financial Analysis: To analyze cash flow characteristics for tenants who opt-in to our financial services.
  • Communication: To send you updates, notifications, and marketing materials (with your consent).
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations.
  • Fraud Prevention and Security: To detect and prevent fraud, and to ensure the security of our Platform.

3. Lawful Basis for Processing

We process your personal information based on the following lawful grounds under POPIA:

  • Contractual Necessity: To fulfill our contractual obligations to you (e.g., providing the Platform services as outlined in our Terms of Service).
  • Legitimate Interests: To pursue our legitimate business interests (e.g., improving the Platform, preventing fraud, conducting research and development).
  • Consent: Where required by POPIA, we will obtain your explicit consent before processing your personal information (e.g., for marketing purposes, collecting conduct information, employment and affordability information, processing identification documents).

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We may disclose your personal information to the following third parties:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the Platform (e.g., payment processors, cloud storage providers, IT support, legal advisors). We have agreements in place with these providers to ensure they comply with POPIA.
  • Landlords and Tenants: We may share information between landlords and tenants as necessary to facilitate the rental process, including contact information, lease details, and payment history.
  • Financial Institutions: With your consent, we may share your financial information with financial institutions to assist you in obtaining financial products (e.g., mortgages).
  • Legal Authorities: We may disclose your information to legal authorities if required by law or legal process (e.g., court order, subpoena).
  • Potential Investors or Acquirers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be disclosed to potential investors or acquirers.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, or damage. These measures include:

  • Encryption: We use encryption to protect your data both in transit and at rest.
  • Access Controls: We restrict access to your personal information to authorized personnel only.
  • Data Minimization: We collect only the personal information that is necessary for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy.
  • Regular Security Assessments: We conduct regular security assessments to identify and address potential vulnerabilities.

6. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law. When your information is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

7. Your Rights

Under POPIA, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right of Access: You can request access to your personal information.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your personal information in certain circumstances (e.g., when it is no longer needed for the purpose it was collected).
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: You can request limitations on how we process your information.
  • Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your personal information.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request a copy of your information in a portable format.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where we rely on your consent to process your information, you can withdraw your consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the contact information provided below.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Platform is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
